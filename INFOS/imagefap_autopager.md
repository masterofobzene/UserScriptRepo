### USE CASE:
--------------
It will load the pages of the gallery you visit each under the next creating a "long scroll" experience but has a safeguard of 
loading batches of 10 pages to prevent memory hog peaks. It has a button below that will ask you to load 10 more. When all pages 
are loaded it will say it so you know you reached the end.

### USAGE:
---------------
Just enter any gallery on the site, it will do its thing automatically. Click on the button at bottom to continue loading more.
