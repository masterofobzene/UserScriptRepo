### USE CASE:
--------------
Its more like a joke but someone may find it useful. It removes the comments on game pages that matches strings of phrases
or words that the user inputs. Optionally you can replace the whole comment with a phrase of yours.

### USAGE:
---------------
On each game page you will now get a floating small window with two buttons: "Edit Phrases" and "Edit Replacement".
The only explanation you need is that you put phrases between double quotes and separate them with commas.
