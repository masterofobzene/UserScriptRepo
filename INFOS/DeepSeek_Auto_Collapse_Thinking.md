### USE CASE:
--------------
Hide deepseek thinking process by default.

### USAGE:
---------------
Just install it.


### WHY?:
---------------
DeepSeek has long thinking times and huge walls of text will be outputted into the thinking cluster. There is no option to 
collapse it inside Deepseek so its this, or manually hiding the wall of text every answer.

---------------
[INSTALL LINK](https://github.com/masterofobzene/UserScriptRepo/raw/refs/heads/main/SFW/DeepSeek_Auto_Collapse_Thinking.user.js)
You must have violentmonkey installed for this to work.
